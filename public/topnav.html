How to use:



The home page:


Here you can see an example of what a tree will look like.

Trees


This page shows you all available public trees. To inspect each more closely, or to modify family members of a specific tree, click on one of the members of the required tree.

Start a new tree

Here you can start a new tree by creating your first member. When you start a new tree, you can also specify if ypu want your tree to be public, ie. visible to anyone, or private. If you choose private you will need to create a name and a passphrase to your tree. Make sure you keep a record of this, as you will need it to access your tree!

Access a private tree

If on the previous page you decided to make a private tree, it is on this page you can gain access to it. Simply enter the tree name and passphrase you created for the tree and hit submit.

What can you do to a tree?

  • New Child: Add a new child to an existing member. Due to visualization limitations, you can only add a child to the direct member of the tree (ie. the left-side member of a marriage if the member has a spouse). Just add the name of the parent into the parent name field and then fill the child's information in.
  • New Partner/Spouse: Add a partner to an existing member of the tree. Just fill in the member's name in the partner field, and then add all the information of the partner in.
  • New oldest ancestor: The oldest ancestor of a tree is the one furthest up on the tree. If you want to add their parents, just add a new oldest ancestor. Add the new parent's information into the appropriate fields. No need to fill in the parent/partner field.
  • Delete a member: If you made a mistake and want to get rid of a member, you can do so by deleting the member. Just add the person's name that you want to delete from the tree into the New person name field and hit submit.
